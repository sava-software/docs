## Brand Story

Sava, a name derived from the fusion of Solana and Java, shares its name with Madagascar's Sava Region - a biodiversity hotspot known for its rich rainforests. This inspired the choice of the indri lemur as our logo, as this remarkable creature is native to Madagascar's forests.
